Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how StreamerClipBot ("the Bot", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our service. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

StreamerClipBot collects only the minimum information necessary to operate:

  • OAuth tokens: When you authorize StreamerClipBot to post on your behalf (e.g., to TikTok or YouTube), we receive and store OAuth access and refresh tokens locally on your device. These tokens are never transmitted to any server we operate.
  • Stream data: The Bot accesses publicly available live stream video and chat data from platforms such as Kick. No private user data from viewers is collected or stored.
  • Log data: The Bot may generate local log files for debugging purposes. These logs contain operational information (clip timestamps, upload statuses) and do not contain personal user information.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

StreamerClipBot does not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information of end users
  • Payment or financial information
  • IP addresses or location data
  • Cookies or tracking identifiers
  • Any data from TikTok users who view uploaded content

3. How We Use Information

The OAuth tokens collected are used solely to authenticate API requests to upload content on your behalf to TikTok and YouTube. No other use is made of this information.

4. Data Storage and Security

All credentials and tokens are stored locally on the operator's device. We do not operate cloud servers or databases that store user data. You are responsible for securing your local environment and keeping your credentials safe.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any data with third parties. The only data transmitted to third parties is the video content you have chosen to upload, sent directly to TikTok or YouTube via their official APIs.

6. TikTok API Data

StreamerClipBot uses TikTok's Content Posting API. Data obtained through TikTok's API is used only for the purpose of uploading content and is handled in accordance with TikTok's Privacy Policy. We do not use TikTok API data for advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond content upload.

7. Children's Privacy

StreamerClipBot is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through our service, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

8. Your Rights

Since StreamerClipBot does not collect or retain personal data on servers we control, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete. If you wish to revoke StreamerClipBot's access to your TikTok or YouTube account, you may do so through those platforms' account settings at any time.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page when changes are made. Continued use of the Bot after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at brynesavoy@gmail.com.
